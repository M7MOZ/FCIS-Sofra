egypt-kaware3(cow foot soup)

Cow foot soup to eat your fingers after it
rich, gelatinous, spicy and truly a delight
(gives you massive POWER 🦁)

ingredients

  • 3 1/2 pounds cow foot
  • 1 pound yam or plantain
  • 1 large onion diced
  • ½ cup diced celery
  • 2-3 green onion chopped
  • 3 thyme sprigs
  • 1 teaspoon white pepper
  • ½ teaspoon ground cumin
  • 8-12 ounce carrot sliced
  • 1 tablespoon chopped parsley
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon smoked paprika

Stuffed oysters

It is one of the very popular foods in Turkey. It is clams served on half a shell, mixed with seasoned rice, and lemon juice is placed on it.

ingredients

  • oysters or mussels.
  • 2 cups of Egyptian rice.
  • Seven spices.
  • cumin.
  • olive oil.
  • dry coriander.
  • salt.

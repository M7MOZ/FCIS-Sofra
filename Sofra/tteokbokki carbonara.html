Asian-tteokbokki carbonara

Carbonara Tteokbokki (Ddukbokki) is a popular Korean street food made with cylindrical (tube-shaped) rice cakes and a creamy carbonara-style sauce.

ingredients

  • 20 pieces of rice cake
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 200 grams chicken breasts
  • Half a cup of broccoli
  • Half a cup of milk
  • Half a cup of whipping cream
  • 2 tablespoons hot sauce
  • Half a teaspoon of hot pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Half a teaspoon of black pepper
  • Half a cup of cheddar cheese
  • green onion

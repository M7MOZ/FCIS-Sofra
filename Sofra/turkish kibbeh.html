turkish Kebbah

The Turkish kibbeh, or as it is also called the chi kofta, is one of the most important and famous fast foods that the Turks eat during their day, as it has special shops that sell it in the form of a sandwich.

ingredients

  • Brown bulgur (4 cups) the cup has a capacity of 200 ml.
  • Very hot water (two and a half cups).
  • Grated onion (without liquid) (2 pieces).
  • grated tomatoes ( 3 pcs ).
  • Tomato sauce (1 tablespoon full).
  • Pepper paste (1 and a half tablespoons full).
  • Salt (1 tablespoon).
  • Crushed garlic (1 teaspoon).
  • black pepper (2 tablespoons).
  • Paprika (2 tablespoons).
  • Hot red pepper (1 tablespoon).
  • Sumac (1 tablespoon).
  • Cumin (1 teaspoon).
  • olive oil (1 tea cup).
  • Pomegranate molasses (half a teacup).

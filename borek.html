Borek

Borek or burek is a filled Anatolian, Balkan, Middle Eastern and Central Asian pie made of a thin flaky dough such as filo with a variety of fillings, such as meat, cheese, spinach or potatoes.

ingredients

    Dough Ingredients:

    • 300 grams (two and a half cups) all-purpose flour.
    • Half a tablespoon of vinegar.
    • 2 tablespoons (30 ml) vegetable oil.
    • 1 egg.
    • pinch of salt.
    • 1 cup warm water (or as needed for the dough).

    Filling:

    • small onion chopped.
    • minced meat.
    • chopped boiled potatoes.
    • garlic cloves.
    • spices to taste.
    • You can add tomato paste, chopped parsley, or any other additions you like to the minced meat

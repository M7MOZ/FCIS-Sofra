egypt-molokhia

greenish soup that's soft as mehalabiya
BUT full of garlic strong shot taklia 😉

ingredients

  • 2 frozen packages (or 800 grams) of diced Molokhia
  • 6-8 crushed garlic cloves
  • 4 cups of chicken broth / chicken flavored vegetable broth/equivalent chicken bullion cubes
  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt and then add to taste (if you use bullion cubes,they may already be salty enough)
  • A pinch of fresh ground black pepper.
  • 1 teaspoon white pepper

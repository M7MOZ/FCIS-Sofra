moroccan-pastilla chicken

A known dish in Morocco, particularly Morocco and Algeria, Pastilla (or bastilla) is a savory chicken pie made with crispy, flaky phyllo dough and a tender shredded chicken filling, seasoned with warm North African flavors including Ras El Hanout, ginger, and cinnamon .

ingredients

To prepare the filling:

  • 1 kg chicken thighs (Castaleta), with bones and without skin .
  • 1 kilo white onions, sliced .
  • ¼ cup chopped coriander .
  • 1 cinnamon stick .
  • 10 strands of saffron .
  • 2 teaspoons grated ginger .
  • 1 tablespoon ras el hanout (Moroccan spices) .
  • 2 tablespoons butter .
  • ¼ cup olive oil .
  • 1 tablespoon of salt .
  • 2 tablespoons honey .
  • 2 scrambled eggs .

To prepare the sweetened almonds:

  • 56 grams roasted almonds .
  • 28 grams of powdered sugar .
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon .

To prepare the dough:

  • 6 slices of phyllo dough .
  • ¼ cup clarified butter (animal ghee) .

asian-sushi

Sushi is defined as any dish that is made with vinegared sushi rice. Although you can make sushi without using any fish or raw fish, many kinds of seafood are used in sushi dishes.

ingredients

  • 3 cups sushi rice
  • 3 ¼ cup water

Sushi vinegar mix

  • ½ cup rice vinegar
  • 4 tbsp. sugar
  • 1 tsp salt

Ingredients used in making sushi

  • Smoked salmon
  • Shrimp
  • Crab sticks
  • 1 avocado (thin slices and strips)
  • 1 mango (thin slices and strips)
  • Nori sheets
  • 2 cucumber
  • 2 carrots
  • Philadelphia cheese or any creamy cheese
  • Wasabi paste
  • Pickled ginger
  • mayonnaise
